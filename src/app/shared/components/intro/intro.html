You’re given 9 bubbles containing words that seem unrelated, called “CUES.” Find 3 Cues that share a common KEY word to form a Triad (a Key is always a single, stand-alone word).

For example, the Cues SWING, FLAT, and PRESSURE share the word “tire” (tire swing, flat tire, and tire pressure). It’s a Triad.

More detailed instructions are available by clicking the button within the game.
